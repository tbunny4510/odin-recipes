Pesto Pasta

Description

A rather simple dish, but with great flavor, and tastes good hot or cold.

Ingredients

  • 1(16 oz) package of pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons pesto
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Cooking Instructions

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to
    a roiling boil. Stir in pasta and return to a boil.
    Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender
    yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes.
    Drain and transfer into a large bownl.
  2. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium-low heat.
    Add onion; cook and stir until softened, about 3 minutes.
    Stir in pesto, salt, and pepper until warmed through.
  3. Add pesto mixture to hot pasta; stir in grated cheese
    and toss well to coat.
